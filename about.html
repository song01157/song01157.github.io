About us

Urban Rabbit Studio is a studio which founded in 2019 and worked on the theme of interactive media design. The studio founder is Joseph Z. Weis, the founder of the studio. As the studio of interactive media service, we have been making progress for over two years now. Through time, innovation and creative approaches have been the critical element, as we never stop until the very end. With our studio in Ottawa, our unique workspace has been at the booming end of employee-friendly tasks and customer satisfaction. The type of results that we publish tend to hit the right chords, and our client base will clarify the rest.
